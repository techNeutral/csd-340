Chapter 1: My Story - Brett Fuller

Hello, my name is Brett from Omaha, Nebraska. I have been working in IT for almost 20 years and over time I have come to enjoy the parts of my job that involve programming far more than any of the others. As a result, here I am now attending Bellevue University attempting to learn how to program the correct way, instead of piecing together random pieces of information on the internet to put something together that feels like it is held together with shoe strings and bubble gum.

I am happily married and a father of two young boys. My oldest, Henry, is 7 years old and is really into sports and video games. Hudson, my 4 year old, loves playing with toys and is a natural collector.

I am not currently employed because I knew that if I tried to do work, school, and be a good dad at the same time one of those things would end up getting a lot less attention than the others. I am also pretty sure that because kids do not have solid deadlines it would be my kids and that ended up getting the least attention and did not seem fair to them. So instead I spend a lot of time running my kids back and forth to school, swimming lessons, and helping to coach their baseball / tee ball teams, while just hanging out to watch them in other sports and practices.

For hobbies I like to play a lot of golf, even though I am bad at it. I have always loved video games since I was a small child, but I find it hard to find the time to play them as much as I would like, so most of my engagement with games these days comes from podcasts and online videos. Recently I have become obsessed with pickleball, and while I am struggling to find time to play video games I find myself playing pickleball multiple times a week.
